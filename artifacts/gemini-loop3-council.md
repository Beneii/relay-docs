# Loop 3 Council: Highest-Impact Work — Gemini Council

1. **Supabase RLS Audit & API Security (RLS Audit + Rate Limiting):** As the product matures, the "Heartbeat" endpoints being wide open to abuse and the potential for leaky RLS policies represent the highest risk to production stability and trust. Performing a comprehensive audit of Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) ensures that user data and notification streams are strictly isolated, while adding rate limiting to Vercel API endpoints prevents malicious actors from draining notification quotas or flooding the heartbeat processing logic.

2. **relay.json Inline Validation & Device Management UI:** Reducing "Developer Friction" is key to stickiness and reducing support burden. Surfacing manifest errors directly in the `edit-app` UI and allowing users to see and manage their registered devices (e.g., device name, platform, last seen) in the dashboard removes the two most common "Why isn't this working?" friction points. It empowers vibe-coders to debug their own setup instantly without guessing if the issue lies in their JSON schema or their device's push token registration.

3. **Team/Dashboard Sharing:** This is the primary "Revenue Multiplier" and the biggest leap toward a "Professional" tool. Allowing solo vibe-coders to invite teammates to view a shared dashboard and receive its notification stream creates a network effect within small teams and significantly increases product stickiness. By moving the value from "Personal Alerts" to "Team Operations," Relay justifies the Pro price point and becomes an essential collaborative bridge for builders.
